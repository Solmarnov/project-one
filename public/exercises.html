Pelvic Tilt

Dificulty:Easy

Purpose: To strengthen the lower abdominal muscles and stretch the low back. How to perform a pelvic tilt: Lie on your back. Exhale and tighten your abdominal muscles while pushing your belly button toward the floor and flatten your lower back. Hold the position for 5 seconds. Repeat the pelvic tilt 10 times holding the position for 5 seconds each time. How can I tell if I’m doing the pelvic tilt right? Place your pinky finger on your hip bone and thumb on your lowest rib (same side of your body). As you tighten your abdominal muscles, the amount of space between your pinky finger and thumb should get smaller.
